## Agent Follow-Up SOP

When a lead submits from any channel:

1. Admin receives lead notification (email configured in Vercel env vars)
2. Mike logs into `https://www.askmagicmike.com/admin` to view lead
3. Check: lead grade (A+/A/B/C/D), temperature (urgent/hot/warm/cold), source attribution
4. **A+/A leads** — call within 1 hour. Do not email first.
5. **B leads** — call or email within 4 hours.
6. **C/D leads** — email within 24 hours.
7. Mark lead status `in_progress` when contacted, `done` when complete.
8. Check `source_attribution` on lead detail page — this tells you which UTM/channel brought them. Close the loop with the channel that's working.

**Response script (phone):**
> "Hi [Name], this is Mike Eatmon from Our Town Properties in Wilson. You reached out through Ask Magic Mike about your home at [address]. I wanted to follow up personally — do you have a couple minutes to talk about what you're thinking?"

## Stop Conditions

Pause all traffic promotion if:
- Production returns 5xx errors on `/ask` or `/value` (check Vercel logs)
- Admin dashboard returns 500 or blank (check ADMIN_SECRET env var)
- Supabase `leads` table stops receiving rows after a real submission
- Any CRM sync error rows appear in `crm_sync_log` (check admin → Intelligence → Automation)

Do NOT pause for: zero leads on day 1 (organic takes time), test leads appearing in admin (expected), social posts with low reach.

## Lead Response SLA

| Grade | Max response time | Method |
|---|---|---|
| A+ (urgent) | 1 hour | Phone call only |
| A (hot) | 2 hours | Phone call or text |
| B (warm) | 4 hours | Call or personal email |
| C (cool) | 24 hours | Email |
| D (cold) | 72 hours | Email or ignore |

**SLA sweep** runs every hour and flags breaches in the admin routing page. If a lead goes uncontacted past the SLA, it appears in the breach list.

# Black Diamond Social Usage

Use `/social-preview` for live overlay previews.

## Formats

- 4:5 feed ad: approved visual plate at `/brand/black-diamond/hero-social-4x5.jpg`
- 9:16 story/reel ad: approved visual plate at `/brand/black-diamond/hero-social-story.jpg`

Keep headline and call-to-action copy as editable ad platform text or live HTML overlay. Do not bake marketing copy into the image files.

## UTM Naming

```text
utm_source=facebook
utm_medium=paid_social
utm_campaign=home_value_wilson_nc
utm_content=feed_4x5_black_diamond
```

For story/reel placements, use:

```text
utm_content=story_9x16_black_diamond
```
